Flux is a lightweight, offline-first productivity app that brings your notes, tasks, journals, habits, and events together in one organized workspace. Designed with Material 3, it delivers a clean, modern experience that helps you stay focused without unnecessary complexity.

Whether you're planning your day, documenting ideas, tracking habits, or managing projects, Flux provides the tools you need while keeping your workflow simple and distraction-free.

### ✨ Features

* 📝 **Rich Markdown Editor** with support for checklists, tables, code blocks, LaTeX, Mermaid diagrams, and image attachments.
* 🗂️ **Customizable Workspaces** to organize notes, journals, tasks, habits, and events the way you work.
* ✅ **Task Management** with recurring tasks, reminders, progress tracking, and productivity insights.
* 📔 **Daily Journals** for capturing thoughts, ideas, and memories.
* 📅 **Calendar & Events** to manage schedules and important dates.
* 🔥 **Habit Tracker** with statistics, streaks, and visual analytics.
* 🎨 **Material You Design** built with Material 3, supporting dynamic colors and a polished Android experience.
* 🔒 **Privacy First** with optional biometric app lock and screen protection.
* 📴 **Offline-First** architecture for fast, reliable access to your data anytime.
* ⚡ **Native Performance** built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose.
* 🌍 **Open Source** under the GPL-3.0 license with regular updates and community-driven improvements.

Flux is built for people who want a fast, beautiful, and privacy-focused productivity app that keeps everything in one place.

**Stay organized. Stay focused. Stay in Flux.**
