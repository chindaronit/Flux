Flux is a lightweight, offline-first productivity app. Whether you're planning your day, documenting ideas, tracking habits, or managing projects, Flux provides the tools you need while keeping your workflow simple and distraction-free.

✨ Features

* 📝 Rich Markdown Editor
* 🗂️ Customizable Workspaces to organize.
* ✅ Task Management & Habit Tracker with productivity insights.
* 📅 Calendar & Events to manage schedules and important dates.
* 🎨 Material You Design built with Material 3.
* 📴 Offline-First architecture.
